PHWDG: Website Design

Overview

The Peel Halton Workforce Development Group (PHWDG) is a community based, non-profit corporation. During my internship, my role was to conduct research considering different stakeholders (view here), and design and develop an interactive website on Experiential Learning (EL) on the company's new project on EL in the Peel Halton region, Project ELPH.

May 2018 — August 2018

Website Mandate and Rationale

Before beginning design and research, we presented the website mandate, goals, and low fidelity information architecture and designs to an advisory group consisting of representatives from Peel and Dufferin-Peel School Boards, City of Mississauga and Brampton, University of Toronto, Sheridan College, Walmart Canada, and other employer representatives. The website goals included:

  • Provide a tool that’s user-friendly, informative and practical that helps to facilitate experiential learning initiatives in peel and halton
  • Communicate the importance and benefits of experiential learning to employers, students and faculty in academic institutions
  • Facilitate efficient planning and delivery of experiential learning programs and initiatives for all stakeholders involved
  • Help grow and support enduring mutually-beneficial relationships built through the experiential learning platform

Information Architecture

After the advisory committee meeting, we were advised to keep the website more employer based, and a simple navigation to get to there they are looking for with ease.
