Student Food Planning UX
UX Research & Design, CCT380- Human Computer Interaction | 2019

Throughout the "human computer interaction course", we worked on a semester long team project. My team identified the problem that while students may plan ahead when buying food, wait times are never incorporated into their schedules. Based off this problem, we conducted user observational analysis, task analysis, lo-fiedlity and hi-fidelity prototypes, and conducted various three studio sessions for usability testing of the different prototypes.
