PHWDG: Research & Design

Overview

The Peel Halton Workforce Development Group (PHWDG) is a community based, non-profit corporation. During my internship, my role was to conduct research considering different stakeholders, and help design an interactive website on Experiential Learning (EL) (view here), on the company's new project on EL in the Peel Halton region, Project ELPH.

Role, Tools, & Duration

Web Researcher and Designer
Primary and Secondary Research, Visual Design, Email Marketing and Outreach

Adobe InDesign, SurveyMonkey

May 2018 — August 2018

Project Goals

  • Educate the diferent stakeholders (employers, students, parents, educator's) on what experiential learning is, providing experiential learning opportunities for High School and University/College students, and the co-benefits of engaging for students and employers
  • Provide informative resources that can help employers connect with different educational institutions across Ontario who are in need of emlpoyers willing to onboard internship and co-op students to partake in experiential learning opportunities

    • Research and Process

    I conducted primary and secondary research on labour trends, government funded employment services, experiential learning theories and best practices, and on other key topics. I identified the four key stakeholders and conducted user research on each of them through surveys. My team and I conducted a meeting with an advisory committee consisting of each of the stakeholders to understand what they would like to learn, and the best way to communicate that information.
