Divercity Collection: Marketing Proposal
Online Advertising & Marketing Proporsal, CCT356- Online Marketing | 2019

Working with the startup fashion company, Divercity Collection, my team and I were assigned to develop an online marketing strategy proposal. In this proposal, we conducted a marketplace evaluation (SWOT analysis, Porter's Forces, PESTEL analysis), considered key issues and marketing challenges, and based off of that defined marketing strategies and key objectives. We created a branding guideline to be used for online ads, and created a collection of mock Facebook, Instagram, and Pinterest advertisements.

Currently, I am a teaching assistant for this course, where I am responsible for aiding students in similar class work, while also grading assignments and quizzes.
