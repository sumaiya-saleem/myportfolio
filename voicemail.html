Overview

Experiencing discomfort when trying to access and navigate the current voicemail system, we observed that this is a pain point among the majority of voicemail users. Therefore, we identified the need for a design change from the current audio management system to a visual system that would improve efficiency for users.

Hypothesis

The graphical playback controls of voicemails are more effective than the audio-only management of voicemail.
